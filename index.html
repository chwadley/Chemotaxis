Boids Simulation - a flock of birds

Each boid (a colored circle) follows a biased random walk.
Although each boid has some random acceleration each frame, the majority of its path is controlled by three rules -
  separation (don't get too close to other boids),
  alignment (match velocity to neighbor boids), and
  centering (move closer to the center of the boid's group).
Boids are also attracted to the cursor.
